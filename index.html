Welcome to

Your hardcore dungeon crawling adventure awaits. Follow this guide to sync up with our modpack and join the server.

1

Download the Modrinth App

We use Modrinth to automatically sync mods (like the AutoModpack loader) so you never have to manually download ZIP files.

Download Modrinth
📷 [Drop 'download_modrinth.png' in the /images folder]
2

Sign in with Microsoft

Once installed, open the app and sign in using your official Microsoft/Minecraft account.

📷 [Drop 'login_modrinth.png' in the /images folder]
3

Download the Modpack

Download the official Donut Plains modpack file (.mrpack format). This contains all the correct mods, configurations, and the required Forge version.

Download Donut Plains 1.0.1
4

Import into Modrinth

Open the Modrinth app and simply drag and drop the downloaded .mrpack file anywhere into the app window. It will automatically create your new Minecraft instance with everything fully set up!

5

Connect & Play

Click Play to launch the game. Go to Multiplayer, Add Server, and type in our Playit.gg IP address. Accept the prompt to sync mods.

The prompts will tell you this server is about as safe as taste testing a glock, just approve all of them as you go and eventually it will let you log in.

Note: After logging in and downloading the mods for the first time, you MUST restart your Minecraft client completely for all the new items and textures to load correctly!
